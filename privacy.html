Privacy Policy

Kidney Tracker · Last updated 14 June 2026

In short: Kidney Tracker keeps everything you enter on your own device. There is no account, no sign-up, and no analytics or tracking. We do not run servers that store your information, and we never sell your data. The only information that leaves your device is what is needed to process a purchase (handled by Apple), and what you choose to export or share yourself.

Who we are

Kidney Tracker ("the app") is developed and provided by Paul Gallagher ("we", "us"). You can contact us at support@gallagher.uk.net.

What the app records, and where it is stored

You can use the app to record things such as fluid intake and output, weight, blood pressure, medications and whether you have taken them, your own blood-test results, reminder times, and basic profile settings such as your daily fluid target and when your day starts.

All of this is stored locally, in a database on your device. It is not uploaded to us or to any third party. We cannot see it. If you delete the app, this data is removed from your device.

No accounts, analytics, tracking, or ads

The app does not ask you to create an account or log in. It contains no analytics or usage-tracking, no advertising, and no third-party tracking technologies. It does not use Apple Health/HealthKit.

Reminders

Reminders are scheduled as local notifications on your device. They are not sent through any server and contain only the neutral reminder text shown in the app.

Reports you create

The app can generate a summary report (for example to show your clinic) as a document on your device. That document is created on-device. If you choose to share or print it, it is sent only to wherever you direct it (e.g. your email, printer, or another app). We do not receive a copy.

Purchases and subscriptions

If you choose to buy a subscription or a one-time purchase, the transaction is processed by Apple through the App Store. We never see or receive your name, card, or payment details.

To manage subscription status and unlock paid features, we use RevenueCat, a purchase-management service. When you make or restore a purchase, RevenueCat processes limited purchase information such as your App Store transaction receipt and an anonymous app-generated identifier. This is used only to determine whether your purchase is active. It is not linked to your name and is not used for advertising.

For more detail, see Apple's privacy policy and RevenueCat's privacy policy.

Children

The app is not directed at children and is intended for use by adults managing their own records, or by a carer on their behalf.

Your control and deletion

Because your data lives on your device, you are in control of it. You can remove individual entries within the app, and removing the app deletes its stored data from your device. We hold no copy to delete on our side.

Not medical advice

The app records and adds up what you enter. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment, and any targets are ones you set yourself based on guidance from your own clinical team. Always follow your healthcare professionals.

Changes to this policy

If we change this policy we will update the date above and post the new version here. Significant changes may also be noted in the app.
